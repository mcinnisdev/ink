| WP Plugin | Ink Replacement | How to Embed | Free? | Ease |

## Recommended: Beehiiv

Beehiiv has a generous free tier, great analytics, and clean embed options. Add the signup snippet once to your Ink base layout template and it appears site-wide.

Newsletter signup snippets added to the Ink base layout automatically appear on every page -- set it once and forget it.
